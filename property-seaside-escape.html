Seaside Escape

Nestled in the heart of the coveted Kitsilano neighborhood, the Seaside Escape stands as a testament to serene coastal living. This detached home offers a tranquil retreat from the hustle and bustle of city life, while still being mere moments away from all the amenities that Kitsilano has to offer.

As you approach the property, you're greeted by a charming facade that exudes coastal charm and timeless elegance. The lush landscaping and manicured gardens create a sense of peace and tranquility, setting the stage for a truly relaxing lifestyle.

Step inside, and you're welcomed into a light-filled sanctuary that seamlessly blends modern sophistication with coastal-inspired design elements. The open-concept layout is perfect for both entertaining and everyday living, with spacious living areas that flow effortlessly from one room to the next.

The heart of the home is the gourmet kitchen, where culinary enthusiasts will delight in the high-end appliances, designer finishes, and ample storage space. Whether you're hosting a dinner party or simply enjoying a quiet meal at home, this culinary haven provides the perfect backdrop for your culinary adventures.

Retreat to the luxurious master suite, where a serene ambiance awaits. Featuring a spacious layout, a walk-in closet, and a spa-like ensuite bathroom, this private sanctuary offers the perfect escape from the stresses of everyday life. Two additional bedrooms provide comfortable accommodations for guests or family members, each offering ample space and natural light.

Outside, the allure of coastal living beckons. Step into the backyard, where a tranquil oasis awaits. Lounge on the patio and soak up the sun, dine al fresco beneath the stars, or simply enjoy the peace and quiet of your own private retreat. With its serene ambiance and stunning coastal vistas, Seaside Escape offers a lifestyle beyond compare in the heart of Kitsilano.
