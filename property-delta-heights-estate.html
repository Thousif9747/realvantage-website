Delta Heights Estate

Situated on the exclusive Tranquil Terrace, Delta Heights Estate stands as a beacon of refined living and architectural excellence. This magnificent property offers a perfect blend of modern luxury and timeless sophistication, set against the backdrop of Vancouver's stunning natural beauty.

The estate's impressive facade showcases meticulous attention to detail, with premium materials and thoughtful design elements that create an immediate sense of grandeur. The professionally landscaped grounds provide a serene setting that complements the home's elegant architecture.

Inside, the home reveals a masterfully planned layout that maximizes both space and functionality. The grand foyer leads to expansive living areas featuring high ceilings, custom millwork, and large windows that showcase the property's beautiful surroundings. The formal dining room and adjacent living spaces are perfect for both intimate gatherings and large-scale entertaining.

The gourmet kitchen is a chef's dream, equipped with professional-grade appliances, custom cabinetry, and a large center island with seating. The adjacent family room features a cozy fireplace and direct access to the outdoor entertaining areas, creating a seamless indoor-outdoor living experience.

The master suite occupies its own private wing, featuring a sitting area, walk-in closets, and a luxurious ensuite bathroom with a soaking tub, separate shower, and dual vanities. Additional bedrooms are generously proportioned and include their own ensuite bathrooms, ensuring comfort and privacy for all residents.

The outdoor spaces are equally impressive, featuring multiple patios, mature landscaping, and stunning views. Whether you're hosting a summer party or enjoying a quiet evening outdoors, Delta Heights Estate provides the perfect setting for luxury living in Vancouver's most prestigious neighborhood.
